Can chaos create connection? Loki, the trickster, cuts Sif’s golden hair not out of hate, but for the thrill of chaos and disruption. Yet from this cruel joke comes something unexpected: new gifts for the gods, made by dwarves, and the forging of Mjolnir. Loki’s story reminds us that even mischief can ignite connection when storytelling turns consequences into shared meaning. Through myth, even betrayal becomes a thread that ties a community together.
